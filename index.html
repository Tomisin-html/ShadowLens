ShadowLens

ShadowLens, a subsidiary of Umbra Media Productions, was founded with a mission to make learning of photography easy and accessible to all. Seeing the difficulties in mastering the art and the price standard media schools charge students, the founders of shadowlens decided to make a way the knowledge would be brought to all, chaper and yet qualitative.

With ShadowLens, prospective students can learn all it takes to be a pro in photography without denting their pockets. The courses are made easy to understand and are flexible. Students can also choose part time to accomodate their other activities. We utilize both physical and online class, with adequate facilities, qualified teachers and a hands-on aproach; more practical. Students are sure to have an amazing learning time with us.
